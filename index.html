Who are we? Why are we different?

We are people interested in technology and making inventions.
We get together for a few hours every week to learn new skills and implement them.

AVRs and PCBs

We are probably the only MakerClub in the world which utilised using of microcontroller and self designed PCBs instead of development boards or modules.

3D Designing

We are also actively using 3D CAD programs, such as SolidWorks. We have two in-house FDM 3D printers. We have access to other five 3D printers that belong to club members, which include an SLA machine.

Our Vision

We are to spread the skills for manufacturing and engineering. We are working on taking our ideas from prototyping to mass manufacturing. We aren't a club where easy things happen.
