FRC Animation Jam

By Egemen Ertem and Ege Özgül

Harsh days for animators

The FRC competiton required our UAA school robotics team to produce a 30 seconds long animation. Just before the semester break, we had a long exam week; Eventually we were worn out to death. Just after the exam week we had recalled -Thanks Büşra hoca- that we had only three days to submit our nonexistent animation to FRC website. So we started super fast and we worked nonstop for three compete days. Egemen drew the assets and Ege builded prepared the scene for the rendering phase. Perfect collobration! After waiting for 5 hours to render the animation, we ended up with a crashed computer and 6 hours of time in 2 in the morning. So we used a computer with a server microprocessor in it and got it rendered in 4 hours, 1.5 hours before the tighest deadline I had ever had.
